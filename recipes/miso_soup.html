Miso Soup

Miso Soup is a soup flavored with Miso Paste and usually includes Tofu and vegetables. It is a very essential dish of Japanese cuisine, and comes with any traditional Japanese meal.

Ingredients

  • 4 cups of dashi
  • 1 package of tofu
  • 4-5 Tbsp of miso paste
  • 2 green onions chopped fine

Instructions

  1. Boil Dashi. Slice green onions very thinly. Cut tofu into 1/2" cubes and add to Dashi.
  2. Reduce heat to low and dissolve miso paste in the Dashi. Take care not to boil.
  3. Add green onions and remove from heat.
